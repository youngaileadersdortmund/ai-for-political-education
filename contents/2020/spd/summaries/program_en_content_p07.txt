ECONOMY PROGRAM  FOR THE 2020 LOCAL  ELECTIONS8 Promoting craftsmanship Dortmund's  skilled  trades  are tradition  and, above  all, the future.  No sector  achieves  more in terms of inclusion,  integration  and innovation  in  everyday  life. In order to promote  local trades,  we want to further  develop  the awarding  of municipal  contracts  - through  new objectives  and significantly   simpler  processes.  If this quicker,  it is not only good for the companies,  it will also be quicker  to implement.
